Withdrawal Information

 The FYBA issues refunds to players who withdraw from the league based on the date the withdrawal is requested. Requests received during the Early Refund Request period will granted a refund of amounts paid minus a non-refundable fee of $10.00 (players who have made a school or AAU team will not be charged the nonrefundable fee). Requests received during the Late Refund request period will be granted a refund of amounts paid minus the non-refundable fee and the uniform fee (total of $40). No refunds will be given after Dec. 31st . Players wishing to withdraw from the league must do so through the league website. Withdrawal requests must be received by the appropriate dates to qualify for a refund.
